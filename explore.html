Popular Destinations

San Thome Basilica Church

Santhome Church in Chennai is one of the oldest churches in India. The Basilica of Saint Thomas Cathedral Basilica, sits on the tomb built over the mortal remains of one of the twelve Apostles of Jesus Christ.

Victoria Public Hall

Victoria Public Hall, also known as Town Hall, is a historical landmark built in honor of Queen Victoria’s Golden Jubilee, showcasing Indo-Saracenic architecture.

Thanjavur Periya Kovil

The Thanjavur Periya Kovil, officially known as the Brihadeeswarar Temple, is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and a masterpiece of Dravidian architecture.
