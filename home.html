Thiruvalluvar Statue

Thiruvalluvar Statue is a towering monument dedicated to the celebrated Tamil poet and philosopher Thiruvalluvar. Standing at 133 feet, symbolizing the 133 chapters of the Thirukkural.

Shore Temple

The Shore Temple is a stunning UNESCO World Heritage site located on the Bay of Bengal coast in Mahabalipuram, Tamil Nadu. Built during the 8th century by the Pallava dynasty.
